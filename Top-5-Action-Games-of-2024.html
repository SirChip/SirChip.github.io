1. Cyberpunk 2077: Phantom Liberty

Developer: CD Projekt Red
Publisher: CD Projekt
Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, PC
Release Date: September 26, 2023

Cyberpunk 2077: Phantom Liberty is an expansion to the base game that brings new life to Night City. With improved gameplay mechanics, a gripping spy-thriller storyline, and the addition of Idris Elba to the cast, this expansion offers intense action and deep narrative experiences in a richly detailed cyberpunk world.

2. God of War: Ragnarök - Valhalla

Developer: Santa Monica Studio
Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
Platforms: PlayStation 5
Release Date: March 15, 2024

God of War: Ragnarök - Valhalla continues the Norse saga of Kratos and Atreus. This standalone expansion builds on the critically acclaimed God of War: Ragnarök, offering new combat mechanics, epic boss battles, and a deeper exploration of Norse mythology. The game pushes the boundaries of action-adventure gaming with its cinematic storytelling and visceral combat.

3. Devil May Cry 6

Developer: Capcom
Publisher: Capcom
Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, PC
Release Date: June 7, 2024

Devil May Cry 6 brings the stylish action series to new heights. Featuring Dante, Nero, and a new playable character, the game offers more diverse gameplay than ever. With its signature blend of swordplay and gunplay, enhanced by next-gen graphics and physics, Devil May Cry 6 delivers an unparalleled action experience.

4. Spider-Man: Venom

Developer: Insomniac Games
Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
Platforms: PlayStation 5
Release Date: October 4, 2024

Spider-Man: Venom is the latest installment in Insomniac's Spider-Man series. Players take control of both Spider-Man and Venom in this action-packed adventure. The game features an expanded New York City, new web-slinging mechanics, and a darker storyline that explores the complex relationship between hero and anti-hero.

5. Bayonetta 4

Developer: PlatinumGames
Publisher: Nintendo
Platforms: Nintendo Switch 2
Release Date: December 13, 2024

Bayonetta 4 continues the over-the-top action series with more outrageous combos, stylish moves, and epic boss fights. Utilizing the power of the next-gen Nintendo console, the game offers smoother gameplay, more detailed environments, and an expanded combat system that pushes the boundaries of the hack-and-slash genre.
